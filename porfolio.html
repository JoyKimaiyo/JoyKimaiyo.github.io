Data Cleaning and Exploratory Analysis

For data cleaning and exploratory analysis, I used pandas in Python. I find pandas easier to use when checking for null values and duplicates and exploring data for better understanding and effective analysis. The data contains zero null values and 1 duplicate which .drop_duplicates() was used .

Data Visualization

  • What are the overall sales trends, and how have they evolved? Are there any significant fluctuations that need to be addressed?
    • A line chart is used to analyze sales trends over time. The order date (year) is placed on the x-axis, while sales are plotted on the y-axis.
    • The visualization reveals fluctuations in sales during 2012, followed by a steady increase from 2013 to 2014.
  • Which product categories contributed the most to the companyâ€™s sales? Which categories are underperforming, if any?
    • A pie chart is utilized to determine category contributions. The category field is assigned to the legend, and sales are mapped to the values.
    • The visualization indicates that office supplies contribute the least to sales, whereas technology represents the highest contribution.
  • How does sales performance vary across regions? Are there promising geographical regions or areas requiring improved marketing?
    • A bar chart is used for regional sales performance analysis. The region field is assigned to the x-axis, and sales are plotted on the y-axis.
    • The visualization highlights that the West region performs the best, while the South has the lowest performance.
    • The East region ranks second in sales performance, showing potential for growth.
  • Which products are more profitable, and which are not?
    • A multi-row card visualization is used to evaluate product profitability.
    • The subcategory and profit fields are added to the card, a title is set in the format tab, and the data is sorted in ascending order.
    • The visualization reveals that accessories are the most profitable products, whereas tables generate no profit.
  • With the available data, what factors affected the companyâ€™s profit?
    • Ship mode analysis indicates that standard shipping yields the highest profit, while same-day shipping contributes the least.
    • Customer segment analysis shows that the consumer segment generates the highest profit.
    • State-wise profit analysis reveals that California produces the highest profit.
    • Seasonal variation analysis demonstrates that quarters three and four of each year consistently generate the highest profits.

Operational Efficiency

The operational efficiency was assessed by calculating the average delivery time. Using a calculated column, the difference between the order date and ship date was explored. Next, we add a card visualization, drag the average delivery time to the fields, and find that the companyâ€™s average delivery time is almost 4 days.
